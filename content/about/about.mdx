I'm Nedhir. I build software and occasionally write about it.

Most of my work involves systems that process data at scale — pipelines, APIs, the infrastructure-adjacent layer that nobody sees but everybody depends on. I care about correctness, clarity, and things that work reliably without needing attention.

## this site

A place to think out loud. Not a portfolio — I don't have a contact form or a "hire me" section. More of a notebook that happens to be public.

The sections:

- **projects** — things I've built or am building
- **devlog** — notes from the process
- **thoughts** — ideas that needed writing down
- **life** — everything else
- **now** — what I'm currently focused on

## elsewhere

I'm mostly offline. If you need to reach me, you know where.
