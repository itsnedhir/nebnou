## what it is

A personal site built with Next.js 14, TypeScript, and Tailwind. Content lives in MDX files processed by contentlayer2. No database, no CMS — just files and a build step.

The design is intentionally minimal: monospace font, dark background, green accents. Somewhere between a terminal and a notebook.

## stack

- **Next.js 14** — App Router, static generation
- **contentlayer2** — MDX as a typed data layer
- **Tailwind CSS** — utility-first, Courier New everywhere
- **Deployed on Vercel**

## why

I wanted a place that felt like mine. Most portfolio sites look the same. This one doesn't try to impress — it just tries to be honest.
