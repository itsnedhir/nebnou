Spring showed up properly this week. The kind of light that makes you feel like you've been indoors too long — which I have.

Been cooking more. Simpler things: eggs, rice, whatever's in the fridge. There's something clarifying about a meal that takes fifteen minutes and produces no leftovers.

## reading

Midway through *The Dispossessed* by Ursula K. Le Guin. It's the kind of book that makes you think differently about ownership, scarcity, and what "enough" actually means. Not sure I have answers yet, but the questions feel sharper.

## the small things

- Switched coffee to after noon. Sleeping better.
- Started going outside before opening the laptop. Small change, noticeable difference.
- Wrote more than I shipped. That balance needs fixing.

Some months feel like setup. This one did. Let's see what April builds toward.
